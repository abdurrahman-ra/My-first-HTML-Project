Third: My Favorite Books

To be honest, I'm not a big fan of reading so I will just put one book.

3.a: Dr. Muhammad Taha – (Dangerous Relationships) علاقات خطرة

Dr. Muhammad Taha is a psychiatrist who made this book to discuss the different kinds of relationships psychologically and their impact on us, it's very self awaring with fully rich content, The abstract of the book is as follows:

Maybe human relationships is the most complex thing ever. Not only most complex.. No.. Yet the most dangerous and definitely the toughest. Human relationships can be one of the most important reasons for happiness, cheer and passion for living. Sometimes it can be for the main source of misery and pain. A relationship can lift you up to the 7th heaven, and another one can slam you down to the 7th earth.. What deforms, tears & hurt is a relationship .. And what fixes, changes & heals people is a relationship.. We grow up through our relationships with each others
– Dr. Muhammad Taha
